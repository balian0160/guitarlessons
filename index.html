About me

MM1066 (also known as The Musicman) is a guitarist from Suffolk, England. He began to learn to play guitar in 2005, and ever since then it has become a passion. In 2007, he created a YouTube account (http://www.youtube.com/musicman1066) and began posting cover videos of songs from popular Metal songs, most of which now have skyrocketed to over 100,000 views each. As his audience and fan base grew, he began writing original music in 2009, and posted his debut single "Memories Lost". This song gained positive response gaining over 500,000 views, and thus inspired him to create more original material and post it online. In 2010, he wrote and recorded two more original songs, "The Unseen" and "Obscurity", both of which gained positive acclaim. As his technical ability had increased, he decided to rewrite parts of his first original, and thus "Memories Lost II" was released in November, 2010. Currently he has a fifth single set for release in 2011, which makes use of faster paced and more technical riffs, with large melodic sections on the side. His current fan base exceeds 45,000 user subscriptions and over 10 million total video views.
